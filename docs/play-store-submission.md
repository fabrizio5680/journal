# Play Store Submission

Operational checklist for the first Google Play Trusted Web Activity (TWA)
release of Quiet Dwelling.

Policy sources reviewed:

- Google Play Data safety form guidance:
  `https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/10787469`
- Google Play account deletion requirements:
  `https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/13327111`
- Google Play target audience guidance:
  `https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9867159`

| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| Privacy policy URL      | `https://thequietdwelling.com/privacy`                                    |

listing unless the legal pages and app UI are updated to match.

## Target Audience And Content

| Declaration area              | Answer                                                                |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Target audience               | 16+ only. Select only older age bands available in Play Console.      |
| Designed for children         | No                                                                    |
| Families program              | Do not opt in                                                         |
| Ads                           | No ads                                                                |
| User-generated public content | No public sharing or social feed                                      |
| Medical/health claims         | Avoid therapy, diagnosis, treatment, crisis, or medical-device claims |

Screenshots and descriptions must not imply the app is for children or for
medical/therapy use. Mood features may be described as personal reflection, not
clinical tracking.

## Store Listing Guardrails

- Say: "Your entries stay on this device and, if you enable sync, in your own
  Google Drive."
- Say: "No ads. No analytics. No third-party search index."
- Do not say: "Only you can read your entries."
- Do not say: "End-to-end encrypted" or "encrypted journal" unless the product
  actually implements E2E encryption where Google cannot read synced Drive data.
- Do not imply medical, counselling, crisis-support, or guaranteed mental-health
  outcomes.
- Keep availability copy to "available in selected countries" until
  `docs/global-market-matrix.md` clears broader markets.

## Pre-Submission Gate

- `npm run build` passes for the hosted PWA.
- `/privacy`, `/terms`, and `/account-deletion` are public and current.
- Legal acceptance, age gate, explicit sensitive-data consent, export, account
  deletion, and consent withdrawal are present in the app.
- `assetlinks.json` contains the local upload signing certificate and the Google
  Play App Signing certificate after the first upload.
- Internal testing confirms the TWA launches without browser chrome.
- Data safety answers have been checked against the live app and privacy policy.
- `docs/ropa.md`, `docs/dpia.md`, `docs/retention.md`,
  `docs/breach-response.md`, and `docs/dsr-procedure.md` are current.
- Google DPA/project region checks and transactional email provider decision are
  recorded before automation for inactive-account warnings or breach emails.
